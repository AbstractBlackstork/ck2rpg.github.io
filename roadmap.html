Procedural Map Generator Roadmap to Beta

Philosophy

Do not generate for the sake of generation. For every feature, ask, "How does it tie in to the game mechanics or planned game mechanics?"

  • Implement performance improvements targeted at being able to generate vanilla size and resolution on 32GB RAM
